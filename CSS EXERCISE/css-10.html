This is a part of design exercise from Ann. Some preliminary practice in plants, colors, senses and architecture. They may seem very shallow, monotonous or even boring. What would a room be like if it breathed, if it could grow? I tried to imagine the possibility. Would it be like a plant, able to feel pain and heal itself after injury? This room is made of plants, it is part of the courtyard, so I named the house 'the garden'.
